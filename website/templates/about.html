    Bio

    My name is Matt. I am from Bellingham but now live in Seattle and I am a web developer. I enjoy both backend and frontend development but when the two sync together is when I start to love my work.

    While studying Computer Science at Western Washington University I became very interested with Machine Learning, Artificial Intelligence and higher mathematics. I became so interested, that I started investing heavy amounts of my free time learning and experimenting with the tools, ideas and mathematics that solve problems that can't otherwise be solved with classical algorithms. I look forward to the day where I write backend tools that use my knowledge of machine learning and front end tools that reflect it.

    Regarding this website, I primarily crafted it to be a platform that lifts all of my projects out of the dark unorganized cave that is my sandbox folder. If you would like to see how I made this site, then feel free to take a peek at the github repository.
